## System Overview

The Hall Management System allows students and faculty members to:
- View available time slots for halls
- Make reservation requests
- Track reservation status
- Manage personal information

**System Features:**
- Real-time availability checking
- Google Calendar integration
- Automated approval workflow
- Payment slip upload
- Email notifications

## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Hall Management System URL
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Dashboard Navigation
After logging in, you'll see:
- **View Free Slots** - Check available time slots
- **My Reservations** - View your reservation history
- **Sign Out** - Log out of the system

## Viewing Free Slots

### Accessing Free Slots
1. Click "View Free Slots" from the main menu
2. The free slots page displays available time slots across different buildings and halls

### Understanding the Interface
- **Date Selection**: Choose your preferred date using the calendar
- **Building Filter**: Filter by specific building (optional)
- **Hall Filter**: Filter by specific hall (optional)
- **7-Day View**: See slots 3 days before and after your selected date

### Reading the Schedule
- **Free Slots**: Displayed as clickable links
- **Busy Slots**: Marked as "Busy" and cannot be selected
- **Time Range**: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM (1-hour slots)

### Making a Selection
1. Select your preferred date from the 7-day view
2. Find your desired hall and time slot
3. Click on any "Free" slot to open the reservation form

## Making Reservations

### Reservation Form Fields

#### Applicant Details
- **Name**: Your full name
- **Student Number**: Your university student ID
- **Mobile Number**: Contact phone number
- **Department**: Your department or association
- **HOD Email**: Head of Department's email for approval

#### Reservation Details
- **Purpose**: Reason for hall reservation
- **Building**: Automatically selected from free slots
- **Hall**: Automatically selected from free slots
- **Hall Details**: Shows hall type and capacity

#### Date & Time
- **Start Time**: Automatically filled from free slots selection
- **End Time**: Automatically filled from free slots selection

### Special Rules
- **A11 301 Hall**: Only available for SE (Software Engineering) students
- **Public Holidays**: System will ask for confirmation if booking on holidays

### Submitting Your Request
1. Fill in all required fields
2. Upload payment slip (if applicable)
3. Agree to terms and conditions
4. Click "Submit Reservation"

## Managing Your Reservations

### Viewing Your Reservations
1. Click "My Reservations" from the main menu
2. View all your reservation requests with current status

### Reservation Statuses
- **Pending HOD**: Waiting for Head of Department approval
- **Pending Dean**: Waiting for Dean approval
- **Pending Admin**: Waiting for administrative approval
- **Approved**: Reservation confirmed
- **Rejected**: Request denied
- **Cancelled**: Reservation cancelled

### Actions Available
- **View Details**: See complete reservation information
- **Cancel**: Cancel pending reservations (if allowed)
- **View Payment Slip**: Download uploaded payment documents

## Payment Process

### Payment Requirements
- Some halls may require payment for reservation
- Payment amounts vary by hall type and capacity
- Both refundable and non-refundable amounts may apply

### Uploading Payment Slip
1. Make payment as specified for your hall
2. Scan or photograph the payment slip
3. Upload the image file in the reservation form
4. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, PDF

### Payment Confirmation
- Payment slip is verified during approval process
- You'll receive email confirmation of payment status
- Keep original payment receipt for records

---

## Approval Workflow

### Multi-Level Approval System
1. **HOD/Head of Department** - First level approval
2. **Dean** - Second level approval
3. **Administrator** - Final confirmation

### Approval Timeline
- **HOD Approval**: Usually 1-2 business days
- **Dean Approval**: Usually 1-2 business days after HOD
- **Admin Approval**: Usually same day

### Email Notifications
You'll receive emails for:
- **Submission Confirmation**: When you submit a request
- **Status Updates**: When your request moves to next approval level
- **Final Decision**: When approved or rejected
- **Reminders**: If additional information is needed

### Checking Status
- Log in to "My Reservations"
- Check real-time status updates
- View approval comments if any

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems
- **Forgot Password**: Contact system administrator
- **Account Locked**: Wait 30 minutes or contact admin
- **Invalid Credentials**: Check username and password spelling

#### Reservation Issues
- **Slot Not Available**: Someone else booked it, try another slot
- **Payment Required**: Some halls need payment, check hall details
- **Validation Errors**: Fill all required fields correctly

#### Technical Issues
- **Page Not Loading**: Check internet connection, refresh browser
- **File Upload Failed**: Check file size and format
- **Email Not Received**: Check spam folder

### Getting Help
- **Technical Support**: Contact IT department
- **Reservation Questions**: Contact Hall Management Office
- **Payment Issues**: Contact Finance Department

### Best Practices
- **Book Early**: Popular slots fill up quickly
- **Check Calendar**: Avoid public holidays and exam periods
- **Complete Forms**: Fill all fields accurately to avoid delays
- **Save Confirmations**: Keep email confirmations for records

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance can I book a hall?
A: You can book up to 30 days in advance, subject to availability.

### Q: Can I cancel a reservation?
A: Yes, you can cancel pending reservations. Approved reservations may require admin approval for cancellation.

### Q: What happens if I need to change my reservation time?
A: You'll need to cancel the current request and submit a new one with the preferred time.

### Q: How do I know if my payment was received?
A: Check your reservation status and email notifications. Payment verification is part of the approval process.

### Q: Can I book multiple halls at the same time?
A: Yes, if they're available and you have proper justification.
